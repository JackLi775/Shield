# Shield - Phone Protection E-Commerce Store

Welcome to Shield! This is your online store for premium phone protection products.

## Project Overview

Shield is a modern e-commerce website selling phone cases, screen protectors, chargers, and charging cables to customers in the USA, Japan, and Europe.

## Current Features

✅ **Homepage** - Professional landing page with hero section
✅ **Product Showcase** - Display of phone cases, screen protectors, chargers, and cables
✅ **Shopping Cart** - Add products to cart and view cart items
✅ **Responsive Design** - Works on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices
✅ **Navigation** - Easy-to-use menu bar

## How to View Your Website

### Option 1: Use GitHub Pages (Recommended)
1. Go to your repository settings: https://github.com/JackLi775/Shield/settings
2. Scroll down to "GitHub Pages"
3. Select "Deploy from a branch"
4. Choose "main" branch
5. Click Save
6. Your website will be live at: `https://jackli775.github.io/Shield/`

### Option 2: Download and Open Locally
1. Download the files to your computer
2. Open `index.html` in your Safari browser
3. You can test the website locally

## How to Use the Website

1. **Browse Products** - Scroll down to see phone cases, screen protectors, chargers, and cables
2. **Add to Cart** - Click "Add to Cart" button on any product
3. **View Cart** - Click the "🛒 Cart" button in the navigation bar to see your cart summary and total price
4. **Navigate** - Click menu items to jump to different sections
